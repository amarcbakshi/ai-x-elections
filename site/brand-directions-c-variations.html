Eagle AI has 100,000 voter challenges pending in Georgia. The QR code tabulation deadline hits July 1. Polling places are consolidating without impact analysis. Election officials have no professional publication. Until today.
AI × Elections is a bi-weekly practitioner newsletter for the county and state administrators who run American elections. Fourteen issues from June 2 through November 10, distributed through civitech.io and the BallotReady network to officials nationwide.

The editorial frame is not partisan. Every major threat to election integrity in 2026 has an AI dimension: algorithmic voter roll challenges, AI-generated disinformation targeting officials directly, AI-powered GOTV operations creating polling place confusion. The frame gives the publication license to cover anything election-relevant without triggering the partisanship filters that cause officials to tune out.
