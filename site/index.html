Who Reads This

AI × Elections is written for professionals who need to understand this intersection as part of their work — not as spectators, but as practitioners who will be asked about it, advise on it, or act on it.

  • Election officials and administrators
  • Campaign technologists and strategists
  • Election security researchers
  • Policy staff and legislative counsel
  • Journalists covering elections or AI
  • Civil society and advocacy organizations
  • Technology companies operating in this space

How We Distribute

The newsletter is free. Distribution begins with a curated seed list drawn from the election administration, campaign technology, and policy communities. Referrals and partnerships with election law firms, academic centers, and civil society organizations accelerate growth.

By end of Year 1, we target 100,000 subscribers across the policy, technology, and practitioner communities who are shaping — or being shaped by — the AI × Elections moment.

Paid sponsorship from election technology vendors, law firms, and foundations opens no later than Issue 4.
